About the Game

Stonehollow: Echoes Below invites players to explore a layered underground realm shaped by time, erosion, and forgotten history.

What starts as basic caves opens up into these huge rooms and forgotten hallways that'll mess with your sense of direction. There is no single path forward.

Players move at their own pace, examining surroundings, activating ancient mechanisms, and uncovering routes that were never meant to be found again.

The Underground World

Living Stone

The caverns are not static. Subtle environmental changes — shifting rock formations, falling debris, or newly revealed openings — alter how areas connect over time. What was once a dead end may later become a crucial pathway.

Traces of a Lost Civilization

Carved walls, broken statues, and remnants of structures suggest that the underground was once inhabited. These details provide narrative context without direct exposition, allowing players to interpret the past through visual clues.

Puzzle & Navigation Systems

The puzzles are built right into the world - they're not just random challenges thrown at you. Players manipulate stone mechanisms, align structures, and uncover hidden routes to progress deeper underground.

  • Rotate ancient stone rings to open passages

  • Activate pressure platforms to stabilize bridges

  • Use vertical movement to access upper ledges

  • Observe environmental patterns to reveal secrets

Atmosphere & Visual Tone

The visual identity should emphasize scale, texture, and depth.
Surfaces range from smooth worn stone to jagged formations, while lighting varies from near darkness to soft natural illumination filtering from above. Even though you're alone down there, it should feel amazing rather than claustrophobic — like discovering a forgotten monument rather than entering a hostile place.

Exploration Progression

Players descend through multiple layers, each with its own character:

  • Natural caverns

    Wild spaces carved by the patient hand of water and eons of time.

  • Structured Halls

    Symmetrical monuments built with ancient and flawless geometric precision.

  • Collapsed Zones

    Navigate through unstable paths and vertical drops into the dark abyss.

  • Deep Chambers

    The silent foundations of the world holding the oldest forgotten ruins.
